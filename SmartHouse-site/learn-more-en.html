Goal

Website for an innovative house that works with voice commands. This web site contains what can be the help in the future, everything is achieved with a voice only. Very convenient and without any problems you can have a helper that is built into your house. SMART HOUSE is very useful for both normal and disadvantaged people. Anyone who is interested and wants to automate a home is welcome to our site. They are cheap, easy for programming and common parts in an online store. On our site you can find steps for assembling, installing and programming the arduino and other parts. SmartHouse options are quite a few. Examples of these are the lights on and off, the window to open and close, to see how many degrees are in your home and more interesting facilities. The special thing is that you can use a portable microphone that is connected to your phone, because the use of voice commands requires a Google Assistant who listens to you. all commands are in English and are recommended to be short.

Implementation

There are a number of Arduino-compatible ESP 8266 modules in the SMART HOUSE that are programmed with the Arduino software environment. These are related to the Adafruit online service, which receives and sends information to them. This information also communicates with Google Assistant . The elements we work with are converted into objects from one, such as the LED plays the role of a normal light bulb in the home, and some motors are also connected to a mechanical part. fi at home or on the mobile web. This site is powered by Visual Studio Code.
